A hackathon is an event where programmers, designers, and other tech enthusiasts collaborate intensively over a short period, typically 24-48 hours, to create innovative projects, solve problems, or build prototypes. Participants form teams and work on projects that they present at the end of the event.

Our hackathon is open to anyone interested in technology and innovation, including students, professionals, hobbyists, and beginners. No prior experience is necessary, and we encourage participants from diverse backgrounds to join.

Participants should bring their own laptops, chargers, and any other equipment needed for their projects. Don't forget to bring ID for registration, comfortable clothes, and any personal items you might need for the duration of the event.

Teams can be formed before the event or during the opening session. If you don't have a team, don't worry—we will have team formation activities and networking opportunities to help you find teammates. Teams usually consist of 3-5 members.
